Our Approach

The MedGuard Safe-Guardian

A zero-friction, camera-first web application designed specifically for the accessibility needs of Indian seniors and their caregivers.

1
Photograph
Seniors snap a single photo of their current pill strips. No typing required.
2
Identify
Advanced vision OCR detects Indian brand names and maps them to chemical generics.
3
Warn
Real-time interaction check queries FDA databases to detect contraindications.
4
Voice Alert
Spoken guidance plays in the senior's native Indian language with clear color codes.
